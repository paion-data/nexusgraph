### 编辑器滚动条滞留

#### 问题详情

进入app页面，有滚动条和白边滞留现象

![editor-scrollbar-stuck-issue](../../../../docs/development/img/editor-scrollbar-stuck-issue.png)

#### 解决方案详情

打开通用设置，找到”**显示滚动条**“设置

![open-general-settings](development/img/open-general-settings.png)

选择”**根据鼠标或触控板自动显示**“按钮

![scrollbar-automatically](development/img/scrollbar-automatically.png)

或者选择“**滚动时**”按钮

![scrollbar-when-scrolling](development/img/scrollbar-when-scrolling.png)
